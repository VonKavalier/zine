---
title: "Alternating: a poem"
---

Languidly the cat lies on the floor
Yet it speedily retreats from
A grown man’s violent swats

Torrentially the flood sweeps away a car
Yet rain sweetly cascades down
The awaiting crops of the farmer

How hot the sun is
Yet in the night
I long for it
